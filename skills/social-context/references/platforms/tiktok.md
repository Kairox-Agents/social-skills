# TikTok — Platform Deep Dive

*Last updated: March 2026*
*Sources: TikTok Creator Portal, Socialync analysis, WordStream, Buffer, community testing data*

## Algorithm Architecture (January 2026 Update)

TikTok updated its algorithm with major changes to content distribution in late 2025/early 2026.

### Distribution Flow
1. **Follower test:** Your video first shows to a small test audience of **your followers**
2. **If followers engage:** Goes wider to non-followers in your niche
3. **If non-followers engage:** Enters broader FYP distribution
4. **Can resurface:** Unlike most platforms, TikTok videos can go viral days or weeks after posting

**The biggest 2026 shift:** Follower engagement is now central to distribution. If your followers don't engage, your video dies before reaching the FYP.

### What the Algorithm Predicts
For each viewer, TikTok predicts:
- Will they watch the full video?
- Will they rewatch it?
- Will they share it?
- Will they save it?
- Will they comment?
- Will they follow the creator?

## 7 Key Signal Changes (2026)

### 1. 70% Completion Rate Required (Up from 50%)
- Need 70%+ completion rate to go viral in 2026 (was 50% in 2024)
- Hooks need to be stronger, pacing faster
- Every second counts — trim ruthlessly

### 2. Rewatch Rate Multiplies Reach
- Videos that get immediately replayed receive massive algorithmic boosts
- **Rewatch rate over 15-20% = strong signal**
- One viewer watching 3 times beats three viewers watching once
- Design for "wait, what?" moments that trigger replays

### 3. Longer Videos Are Winning
- **60-180 second videos now outperform 15-second clips**
- TikTok competing with YouTube → longer videos = more ad inventory
- Don't make videos short to be short; make them as long as they need to be engaging
- Story-based content excels in longer formats

### 4. Original Audio Gets Priority
- TikTok rebalanced to reward original audio more heavily
- Trending sounds still work but original audio gets a boost
- Use trending sounds within first 24 hours or create your own
- Voiceovers and original commentary are rewarded

### 5. TikTok Is a Search Engine
- **64% of Gen Z search on TikTok like Google**
- SEO matters: captions need keywords, on-screen text needs to be searchable
- Your hook should answer a question people search for
- Think: "How to..." / "Why does..." / "Best [thing] for [purpose]"

### 6. Engagement Velocity in First Hour
- How quickly followers respond determines distribution speed
- Fast engagement in first hour = wider reach
- Post when your audience is most active
- Be available to reply to comments immediately after posting

### 7. Watch Time Over Everything
- Algorithm heavily favors videos watched in full or rewatched
- A 15-second video watched fully beats a 60-second video watched halfway
- Retention > length, always

## Ranking Signal Weights (Estimated)

| Signal | Weight | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Completion rate** | Highest | 70%+ threshold for viral distribution |
| **Rewatch rate** | Very High | 15-20%+ = strong signal |
| **Save-to-like ratio** | Very High | High saves relative to likes = strong quality signal |
| **Shares (DM + external)** | High | Especially DM sends |
| **Comments** | High | Especially replies from creator |
| **Follow-after-view** | High | Strong signal of creator value |
| **Watch time (total)** | Medium-High | Longer watch = more valuable |
| **Likes** | Medium | Less powerful than saves or shares |

### Negative Signals
- "Not interested" swipes
- Scroll-past (immediate swipe = worst signal)
- Video not watched past first 3 seconds
- Same content posted across platforms detected (especially with watermarks)

| Element | Spec |

## Content Strategy (2026)

### What Works Now
- **Hook in first 3 seconds** — bold statement, question, visual pattern interrupt, text on screen that creates curiosity
- **Beat structure:** Hook → tension → value → payoff → CTA
- **CTA spoken mid-video** (not at end — drop-off too high by then)
- **Searchable captions** with main keyword + description + CTA
- **Consistency:** 3-5 posts/week maintains algorithm favor
- **Original audio/voiceover** → algorithmic boost

### What No Longer Works
- ❌ 15-second videos just to hit a "sweet spot" (longer is now better)
- ❌ Trending sounds without adding value
- ❌ Clickbait hooks that don't deliver
- ❌ Copy-pasting content from Instagram (detected)
- ❌ Watermarks from other platforms
- ❌ Inconsistent posting (algorithm punishes gaps)

### Script Structure (for caption/script writing)
```
Hook (0-3s): Bold claim, surprising fact, or question
Setup (3-10s): Context — why this matters
Value (10-45s): The meat — specific, actionable content
Payoff (45-55s): The key takeaway or twist
CTA (mid-video, ~30s): Spoken naturally, not at the very end
```

## Posting Strategy

### Optimal Times
| Day | Best Windows | Notes |
|-----|-------------|-------|
| Tuesday-Thursday | 2-5 PM, 7-9 PM | Highest activity |
| Monday, Friday | Less competitive | Can work for niche content |
| Timing | Less critical than other platforms | Can resurface weeks later |
*Times in audience's local timezone. But virality doesn't depend on timing as heavily as X or LinkedIn.*

### Frequency
- Minimum: 1/day
- Sweet spot: 1-3/day
- Maximum: 3/day (more = each video competes with your others)
- Consistency matters more than volume

## Voice Guide

- Spoken language, NOT written language read aloud
- Record yourself saying it naturally, then use THAT as the script
- Lowercase, casual punctuation in captions
- Platform slang acceptable ("ngl", "fr", "iykyk")
- Energy and personality > production quality
- Direct to camera works best for educational content
- Show, don't tell — visual proof (real analytics, real product, real results) = authenticity

## Key Gotchas

1. **70% completion is the new threshold.** If average watch isn't 70%+, the video won't get broad distribution.
2. **Followers see it first.** If your followers don't engage, the video never reaches the FYP. Build an engaged follower base.
3. **Longer videos win now.** Stop cutting good content short — 60-180 seconds is the new sweet spot.
4. **Rewatch rate is a multiplier.** Design for "wait, what?" moments. One viewer watching 3x > three viewers watching 1x.
5. **TikTok is a search engine.** Optimize captions and on-screen text for search keywords. 64% of Gen Z search here first.
6. **Original audio > trending sounds.** Trending sounds still work but original voiceovers get algorithmic preference.
7. **Cross-platform content is detected.** Instagram watermarks, identical content across platforms = deprioritized.
8. **Mid-video CTA > end CTA.** Most viewers drop off before the end. Place your CTA at ~60-70% through the video.
