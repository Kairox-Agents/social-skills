# X Algorithm Deep Dive (March 2026)

## High-confidence signals

- Home feed is recommendation-heavy and evaluates candidate posts in staged expansion.
- Early engagement velocity matters heavily.
- Replies are weighted more than likes; reply chains with author participation are strongest conversation signal.
- External links in main post tend to reduce reach vs native content.
- Native media generally improves dwell time and downstream engagement.

## Medium-confidence claims (commonly cited by creator research)

- Author-reply interaction can massively outweigh likes in ranking value.
- Premium accounts often report distribution lift vs non-premium.
- Dwell time and quality interactions are used beyond simple raw counts.

## Practical optimization rules

1. Open with a strong first line (3-8 words).
2. Ask for a specific response (not generic "thoughts?").
3. Keep post scannable with short lines.
4. Keep links out of main tweet; use follow-up reply.
5. Reply quickly to comments in the first hour.

## Anti-patterns

- Hashtag spam
- Obvious engagement bait loops
- Generic motivational copy
- Link-dump posts with no native value
